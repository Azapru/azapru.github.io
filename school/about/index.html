About us

We are a passionate group, focused on creating innovative software and immersive games that captivate and inspire. Our team is a collective of talented developers, artists, and storytellers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology and entertainment.



Our story

Founded in 2016, 000-Group began as a small but ambitious team of university friends who shared a love for coding, gaming, and creative problem-solving. Our journey started in a dimly lit dorm room, where countless hours were spent brainstorming, coding, and dreaming big. Fueled by ramen and late-night energy drinks, we set out to make our mark in the world of software and game development.



Our mission

Our mission is to develop high-quality software and games that not only entertain but also challenge and engage users. We strive to create experiences that resonate with people on a deeper level, blending innovative technology with compelling narratives and stunning visuals. At 000-Group, we believe that every project is an opportunity to tell a story, spark imagination, and bring people together.
