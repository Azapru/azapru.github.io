Custom Songs Guide

============================================

How to make custom song:

- Get ogg file of the song you wanna map, you can convert mp3 to ogg using Audacity

- Open the game and go to "Tools > Create Custom Song" and create the song

- Find your song in song selection and press F7 to open map editor

- Map editor controls: Space - start preview from current section, Left Click - Place object, Right Click - Remove object, Scroll up/down - Go section up/down, Ctrl+S - Save song, Enter - Play test the song

- Enter "Lenght of 1/4 of 1 beat" and "Attacks speed" (attacks recommended speed: 100+)

- Start mapping! Just remember to save before playing!

============================================

How to fix BPM of the song (fix off-sync song):

- Try to change bpm by a little (0.5) until you will have no numbers after dot in "Lenght of 1/4 of 1 beat"

- When you will have good bpm, open original song in Audacity, select all go to Effects > Change Tempo and change bpm from original to your new good bpm.

- Export ogg and done!
