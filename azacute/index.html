Azapru

Genres: Hardcore / Doomcore / Crossbreed


Real name: Karol Grabiński. Aza makes various genres, mainly focusing on creating dark and melodic songs. They started publishing music in 2024, later, inspired by Illegal Wave Records, Cokolwiecore, Morita Yuuhei and Noizenecio... they started azACUTE Records label. Aza is also the main designer of the label.

prycwrn

Genres: Breakcore / Hardcore / EDM / Orchestral


prycwrn (spelled "prycern") is a Romanian artist and musician. Making music ever since kindergarten, they like to experiment and combine genres they like. Often times, their music sounds as if someone is beating you up with a bouquet of flowers, as prycwrn manages to combine and balance harsh sounds with emotional melodies. They also have a crippling mango monster addiction.

MADKSH2 / U.V.B

Genres: Gabber / Early Speedcore / Noisecore / Amigacore


UK based MADKSH2 (mad-kay-es-haitch-too) first discovered Hardcore through the track "Foetus Of Hell" by Aftermath in 2018, ever since that day he fell in love with the simplistic but brutal sound of Early Hardcore. The name derives from the .MADK file format for PlayerPRO in combination with SH2, an acronym for the 2001 horror classic Silent Hill 2.
